/ About me

I'm a student majoring in computing science. I've completed two years of my university life in Zhejiang University, P.R.China and now I'm in Simon Fraser University for rest of my journey to get graduated.


I'm interested in trying all new things, especially in digital world. Thus, I always get passionate about creating new software. I do agree that trying to fix bugs is suffering but nothing can be more satisfying than seeing my own software successfully run on my computer.


Additionally, I love travelling and almost all sports. These two things make me bright and energetic. I'm also a mature piano player with preference of classical music.
